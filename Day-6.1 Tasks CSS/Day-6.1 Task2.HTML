South Korea

South Korea, officially the Republic of Korea, is a highly developed East Asian nation occupying the southern half of the Korean Peninsula, known for its unique blend of ancient history and cutting-edge technology. The capital and largest metropolis, Seoul, is a global powerhouse and home to nearly half the country's population

South Korea's GDP

South Korea has a highly developed, mixed economy, ranking as the world's 14th largest by nominal GDP. The nominal GDP is estimated at approximately $1.85 trillion USD as of 2025, with a GDP per capita of around $35,962 USD. The economy, sometimes referred to as the "Miracle on the Han River" due to its rapid post-war development, is heavily export-oriented and dominated by advanced manufacturing and services, which account for roughly 31.6% and 58.4% of the GDP respectively.

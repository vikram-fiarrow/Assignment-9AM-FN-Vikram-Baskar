Tamilnadu's speciality

Food

Tamil Nadu's food specialities include iconic dishes like sambar, idli, and dosa, along with regional specialities such as Chettinad chicken, Madurai's meat dishes, and Kanyakumari's fish preparations. Other popular items include pongal, rasam, filter coffee, and sweets like banana bonda and payasam.

Pongal

Pongal is a traditional South Indian dish made from rice and lentils, prepared in both sweet and savory versions and is a central part of the Pongal harvest festival.
